Welcome to the (hopefully) largest compendium of Star Trek Ascii-Art going.
Below you will find an assortment of Star Trek logos, equipment, and the good
old ship's themselves.  The art ranges from 'Star Trek - The Original Series'
thru to 'Deep Space Nine' and includes pictures of ships and equipment from most
of the forces featured, e.g. the UFP, Romulans, Klingons, Cardassians, Ferengi.

I hope you like the compendium, and find a good use for it.

A very BIG thankyou goes out to all of those whose work features in this file,
and an even BIGGER apology that your names are not mentioned herein, but due
to disk restrictions, I am trying to keep this file as small as possible.
(Which is getting hard as you all churn out such good work).
Again, a BIG thankyou.  Keep posting y'all!

-=Falcon=-      (ptruman_rowan_cov_ac_uk)       Star-Trek Compendium Keeper
